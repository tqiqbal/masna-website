Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about working at Masna AI

What is the interview process like?

Our interview process typically consists of an initial screening call, a technical assessment or case study, and team interviews. The entire process usually takes 2-3 weeks, and we strive to provide clear feedback and timely updates throughout.

Do you offer remote work options?

Yes, we offer flexible work arrangements including hybrid and remote options for many roles. Some positions require on-site presence due to their nature, which will be clearly indicated in the job description.

What benefits do you offer?

Our benefits package includes comprehensive health insurance, flexible working options, professional development allowance, stock options, paid time off, parental leave, and regular team events and retreats.

What's your company culture like?

We've built a culture that values innovation, collaboration, impact, and continuous learning. We celebrate diverse perspectives and provide an environment where people feel empowered to do their best work while maintaining work-life balance.

I don't see a role that matches my skills. Can I still apply?

Absolutely! We're always interested in connecting with talented individuals, even if we don't have an open role that matches your profile right now. Submit your resume to careers@masna.ai with a note about your interests, and we'll keep you in mind for future opportunities.
