What is Masna?

Masna transforms every business with Universal AI Employees (multi-agent Gen AI applications) who can work alongside human employees. Masna has developed standard employees for common enterprise roles such as Customer Service, Data Analyst, Procurement, Sales Enablement, and more. Masna's unique multi-agent orchestration platform, called the Generative Workflow Engine™, enables the creation of specialized AI employees for automating complex workflows within enterprises. Customers and partners can easily configure or conversationally generate new AI employees. Masna can analyze vast amounts of data, identify patterns, and provide actionable insights, thereby enabling employees to make informed decisions swiftly and accurately. Masna AI's capabilities extend to automating routine tasks, reducing human intervention, and improving overall productivity, making it an invaluable tool for the modern industry.

Is Masna easy to use?

Engaging with Masna is as intuitive as chatting with a colleague. Simply begin asking Masna questions via chat spaces. There is no learning curve or specialized training required.

How does Masna work?

Masna is the missing operating system that makes Enterprise Generative AI possible—simple to implement, accurate, and trusted. Simply ask her to do a complex task, and through her Generative Workflow Engine™, Masna figures out how to break it into simpler tasks and uses enterprise tools to accomplish them. Masna's flexible agent architecture scales to do a large number of tasks out of the box. Masna continuously learns from your best employees and keeps getting better. Additionally, the MasnaFusion™ model combines the best public LLMs and our homegrown smaller private LLMs, offering unrivaled accuracy.

Is Masna accurate?

Masna uses our own 3 trillion parameter mixture of experts model called MasnaFusion™. MasnaFusion™ intelligently leverages the power of a diverse range of foundational models, including GPT-4, Claude, Gemini, Llama3, Mistral, Databricks DBRX, Snowflake Arctic, and even your own custom models. MasnaFusion™ intelligently combines the output from 100s of models to deliver unmatched accuracy and reliability. Masna automatically evaluates and updates the competencies of each model so the results you get are always the most accurate and optimal.

Is Masna secure?

Masna takes the principles of data security and privacy seriously. Masna ensures that all authentication and authorization requirements are met and that employees can only see responses based on what they are authorized to see. Masna's inbuilt privacy vault and data governance layer redacts sensitive information before sharing it with public LLMs.

Is Masna private?

Masna is built with trust at its core, compliant with leading international standards such as HIPAA, GDPR, SOC 2, and ISO 27001. Robust privacy measures including automatic redaction and safe de-identification of sensitive data, audit logs, real-time monitoring, robust encryption of all data at rest and in transit, and explainability across all output results ensure your enterprise data remains private, secure, and compliant. For document generation use cases, Masna also checks for any copyright violations to ensure customers don't incur IP liabilities. Additionally, Masna never trains models on one customer's data to benefit other customers.
